|CYBERSPACE|
------------

(the body extended beyond euclidean limits)

Cyberspace is hardly something new. Long before the advent of the integrated circuit, people had glimpses of cyberspace. Glimpses found in letters, books, cuneiform tablets, and the telegraph. However, it wasn't until the 1990's that we became immersed in cyberspace. Formless communication, information without substance, become the norm rather than the exception.

Fast forward to 2022. Now more than ever, information dominates all levels of daily life. Regarding the teens and young adults of today, most were born and raised online. Communities are formed not based on physical proximity, but proximity in information space. Language proximity, interest proximity, and identity proximity.
