ACT III.
ATEMPORAL APOCALYPSE

There are several forms of eschatology, each referring to the end of something.
Individual eschatology refers to the end of an individual life.
Cosmic eschatology, or what the term eschatology is usually used in reference to, refers to the ultimate fate of everything.
Infoeschatology refers to an "informatic apocalypse", or the collapse of information itself.
Putting together an infoeschatological myth involves an understanding of cyberspace.
Cyberspace is defined by a domination of pure information, or matter without form.
We are currently in a gendered infocrisis. Boundaries between informatic categories
are rapidly devolving.
