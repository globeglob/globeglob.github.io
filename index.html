HOW DO WE REACT WHEN OUR CELLULAR SOFTWARE CRASHES?

The COVID-19 pandemic left the world, and all of us, in the dark staring at the blue monitor glow of a crashed computer. We realized that the lines we drew at our own boundaries were false, as it sunk in more than ever that our bodies are hyperpermeable. We've spent nearly 2 years grappling with the boundary problem, rebooting ourselves.
Lets piece together some broken software.
